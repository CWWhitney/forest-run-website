## Sauna & Wellness

Recover and reconnect with our wood-fired sauna and thermal wellness experiences.

- **Private Sessions**: Available by appointment
- **Duration**: 1 hour
- **Price**: $35/person | $180/group of 5+

- "Heat & Cold for Resilience" (2 hours)

## Overnight Stays

Immerse yourself in nature with rustic, comfortable accommodations.

### The Cabin
- **Sleeps**: 2-4 people
- **Amenities**: Wood stove, hot shower, simple kitchen
- **Price**: $150-200/night
- **Best for**: Couples, small families, overnight training visitors

### Premium Camping
- **Sites**: 2-3 secluded tent sites
- **Each Site Sleeps**: 2-4 people
- **Price**: $50-75/site per night
- **Amenities**: Fire ring, picnic table, composting toilet nearby

### Package Deals
**Weekend Wellness Retreat**: Friday-Sunday  
- Cabin stay (2 nights) + 2 sauna sessions + guided trail run + breakfast
- **$420/couple**

**Training Camp Package**: 2-3 days  
- Group lodging + daily guided runs + sauna/recovery sessions + meals
- **Custom pricing by group size**
