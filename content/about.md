## Our Story

Forest Run Collective was founded with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where people can recover and reconnect with nature. Our 2-acre property sits adjacent to the Camden Snow Bowl and connects to an extensive trail network that draws runners and outdoor enthusiasts from across New England.

### Our Facilities

**Sauna**: Traditional wood-fired design, cold plunge. A sanctuary for recovery, resilience, and connection.

**The Cabin**: A rustic, comfortable 2-person retreat with wood stove, hot shower, and views of the forest canopy.

**Trails**: Direct access to 15+ miles of maintained trails in the Camden Hills for those interested in guided running or hiking.
